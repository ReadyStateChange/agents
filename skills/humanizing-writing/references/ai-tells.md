# AI Writing Tells

Reference list of words, phrases, and structures that often signal AI-generated text.

## Tier 1 Red Flags (usually replace)

- delve
- tapestry (metaphorical)
- paradigm shift
- leverage (verb)
- harness (metaphorical)
- navigate (metaphorical)
- realm
- embark on a journey
- myriad
- plethora
- multifaceted
- revolutionize
- synergy
- resonate

## Tier 2 Suspicious In Clusters

Treat as a signal when 3+ appear in a short piece.

- robust
- seamless
- cutting-edge
- innovative
- comprehensive
- pivotal
- nuanced
- compelling
- transformative
- bolster
- underscore
- evolving
- fostering
- imperative
- intricate
- overarching
- unprecedented

## Significance Inflation

- serves as / stands as / represents a testament
- vital / pivotal / crucial role
- marks a turning point
- reflects broader trends
- symbolizes an enduring shift
- shapes the future

- moreover
- furthermore
- additionally
- that said

## Hedge/Filler Openers

- it's important to note that
- it's worth mentioning that
- while there are certainly challenges
- this is not without its drawbacks
- to be sure
- to be fair
- based on available information
- it could potentially be argued that

## Chatbot Artifacts

- great question!
- certainly!
- absolutely!
- i hope this helps
- let me know if you'd like me to expand

## Pattern Tells (structure-level)

- repeated "not just X, but Y" across multiple paragraphs
- forced triads ("speed, quality, and innovation")
- repeated section endings with "the takeaway"
- repeated headline/list template with identical cadence
- vague attributions ("experts say", "industry observers noted")

## Preferred Fix Strategy

1. Delete inflation before replacing wording.
2. Replace vague claims with specific facts or remove them.
3. Keep one clear term instead of synonym cycling.
4. Vary rhythm and paragraph shape.
5. Preserve factual meaning.
